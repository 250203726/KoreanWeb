In this homework, you have to create the View of your own Portfolio.  

Examples

1. https://www.w3schools.com/w3css/tryw3css_templates_portfolio.htm#portfolio

2. https://www.w3schools.com/w3css/tryw3css_templates_food_blog.htm


As you can see both these examples are simple yet elegant.


Requirement:

1. No Bootstrap, jQuery, JS. Only HTML5 and CSS!

2. The .html and .css files should be easy to read (Indents)

3. Your view should include menu navigation bar, images, etc.


Score Evaluation:

The score will be evaluated following criteria

1. Style and colors are consistent

2. Font size/spacing is easy to read (Adequate text-to-background contrast)

3. Emphasis (bold, italics, etc.) is used sparingly

4. Easy to follow contents
