project

The website is for showing  some data about Korean universities. Ranking part shows the ranking of Korean universities in the order of 2017 QS world universities rankings. There are 29 universities in Korea from the top to the top 1000. In the chart part, you can see the service quality index by schools and years by various charts. The service quality index is a source of KS-SQL, and it is data on education sevice - universities. The schools part provides a description of the university, including the location of the university, the size of the university, the year of establishment, and the number of students. Finally, you can communicate with us anytime through the contact part.
